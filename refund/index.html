Refund Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Free Trial

All plans include a 7-day free trial. No payment is taken during the trial period. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends at no cost.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available within 7 days of your first paid payment only. After the 7-day window, no refunds are issued for the current or remaining billing period.

Refunds are not available on renewal payments, whether monthly or annual.

Annual Plans

Annual plan purchases are eligible for a refund within 7 days of the initial purchase date only.

Renewals of annual plans are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

Email amol@digiconian.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and your account email address. We process eligible refunds within 5–7 business days.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger a refund unless you are within the 7-day refund window from your first paid payment.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed by our payment provider, who acts as Merchant of Record. Refunds are returned to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.
